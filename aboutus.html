About us

Welcome to our team! We are a passionate group of individuals who share a common goal of creating sustainable products that contribute to a greener planet. Our focus is on using used coffee as the main ingredient in our products, because we believe in the power of recycling and repurposing to reduce waste.

At our core, we are driven by a desire to make a positive impact on the environment while also creating high-quality and innovative products. We believe that sustainability and style can go hand in hand, and we are constantly working to create products that are not only eco-friendly, but also beautiful and functional.

Our team is made up of individuals with diverse backgrounds and skill sets, all of whom bring their unique perspectives and expertise to the table. We are united by our shared values of sustainability, creativity, and a commitment to making a difference in the world.

Thank you for joining us on this journey towards a more sustainable future. We look forward to sharing our products with you and hope that they inspire you to make your own positive impact on the planet.
